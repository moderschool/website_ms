SPORTS INFRASTRUCTURE

Modern School offers world-class sports facilities that foster physical development, teamwork, and competitive spirit among students. Our state-of-the-art infrastructure supports a wide range of indoor and outdoor sports activities.

Indoor Courts

BADMINTON

Professional indoor badminton arena featuring BWF-approved synthetic mats. The facility is optimized for shuttlecock trajectory and student safety with specialized lighting and ventilation systems.

SurfaceAnti-skid PVC flooring for joint protection with professional court markings.
EquipmentProfessional-grade nets, posts, and shuttlecocks provided for all practice sessions.
Olympic Standard

BASKETBALL

Equipped with a FIBA-standard high-density synthetic cushioned floor, our basketball arena is built for elite performance and injury prevention. The court features professional lighting for evening matches.

HoopsProfessional breakaway rims with height adjustability for different age groups.
CourtFull-sized court with professional markings and spectator seating for 200+.
Outdoor Synthetic

VOLLEYBALL

Specialized all-weather courts with advanced net systems to support competitive team training and tournaments. The facility includes separate practice areas for serving and spiking drills.

SafetyShock-absorbing zones and specialized non-abrasive surface for dive protection.
EquipmentInternational standard nets, poles, and official match balls for training.
Indoor Arena

TABLE TENNIS

An indoor facility featuring professional-grade ITTF-approved tables and high-grip flooring for tactical gameplay. The arena is equipped with specialized lighting to eliminate shadows and glare.

Tables8 professional tables with consistent bounce and professional net systems.
TrainingRobotic ball machines and video analysis equipment for technique improvement.
Curated Pitch

CRICKET

Our cricket facility features a vast natural turf outfield and dedicated practice bays. The pitches are curated for consistent bounce to help budding cricketers refine their technique under expert coaching.

Practice Bays3 Full-length nets with both turf and cement wickets for varied practice.
Coaching TechAutomatic bowling machines for specialized batting drills and speed training.
FacilitiesPavilion with changing rooms, scorer's box, and spectator seating area.
Synthetic Track

TRACK & FIELD

The 400m synthetic all-weather track is engineered for peak performance, providing optimal traction and energy return for sprinters and long-distance runners alike. The facility hosts annual inter-school athletic meets.

ArenasDedicated pits for Long Jump, Triple Jump, High Jump, Shot-put, and Hurdle sectors.
EquipmentElectronic timing system, starting blocks, and professional landing mats.
Victory & Spirit

SPORTS MEET GLIMPSES

Annual sports events showcase the talent, determination, and sportsmanship of our students. These moments capture the essence of teamwork and competitive spirit that Modern School nurtures.
