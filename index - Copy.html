About Our Institution

Modern School, Kota has been providing quality education for over three decades. Our institution is committed to nurturing young minds and preparing them for the challenges of tomorrow.

Our campus spans over 10 acres with state-of-the-art facilities including modern classrooms, science and computer labs, a well-stocked library, and extensive sports facilities.
