1973

Foundation of Modern School

Modern School was established with a vision to provide quality education that combines traditional values with modern teaching methodologies.

1985

Expansion of Campus

The school expanded its infrastructure with new buildings, laboratories, and sports facilities to accommodate the growing student population.

1998

Introduction of Digital Learning

Modern School became one of the first institutions in Kota to integrate computer education and digital learning tools into the curriculum.

2010

Recognition for Excellence

The school received the "Best Educational Institution" award from the Rajasthan Education Board for outstanding academic performance.

2020

Modern Digital Infrastructure

Implementation of state-of-the-art digital classrooms, online learning platforms, and smart campus initiatives to enhance the learning experience.
